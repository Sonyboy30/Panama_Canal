Welcome to the Panama Canal Museum

The Panama Canal stands as one of humanity’s most impressive engineering feats. Explore how ambition, innovation, and sacrifice shaped its creation and impact on global trade.

Built in the early 20th century, the Canal transformed global commerce by connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. Its story is one of determination, disease, politics, and progress — a symbol of both human ingenuity and imperial ambition.
