Impact of the Canal

The Panama Canal remains a cornerstone of world trade—handling 5% of global commerce. It stands as a monument to human innovation and a reminder of the Progressive Era’s contradictions.

“The canal was not just a triumph of engineering—it was a statement of power.” — National Archives, 2022
